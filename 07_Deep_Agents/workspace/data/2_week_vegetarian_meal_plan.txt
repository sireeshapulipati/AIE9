## Hydration Guidelines
- Drink at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water daily.
- Include herbal teas, especially chamomile and valerian root, in the evening to promote relaxation and better sleep.

## Meal Prep Tips
1. **Batch Cooking**: Prepare grains (like quinoa or brown rice) in bulk at the beginning of the week.
2. **Chop Vegetables**: Pre-chop the veggies for easy access throughout the week.
3. **Use Containers**: Store meals in portioned containers for grab-and-go convenience.
4. **Plan Ahead**: Set aside time each week to go grocery shopping and prep essential ingredients.
5. **Stay Flexible**: Feel free to swap ingredients based on availability or personal preference.
