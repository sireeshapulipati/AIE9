### Energy-Enhancing Vegetarian Foods
1. **Whole Grains**: Quinoa, brown rice, oats, and whole grain bread provide sustained energy.
2. **Legumes**: Lentils, chickpeas, and black beans are high in protein and fiber, stabilizing blood sugar levels.
3. **Nuts and Seeds**: Almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, and flaxseeds offer healthy fats and protein.
4. **Fruits**: Bananas, apples, and berries are great for quick energy and high in vitamins.
5. **Vegetables**: Leafy greens (spinach, kale) and root vegetables (sweet potatoes) are nutrient-dense.
6. **Fermented Foods**: Yogurt and kefir support gut health, which is vital for energy production.

### Sleep-Supporting Vegetarian Foods
1. **Complex Carbohydrates**: Brown rice, oats, and whole grains promote serotonin production.
2. **Nuts**: Walnuts and almonds contain melatonin and magnesium.
3. **Herbal Teas**: Chamomile and valerian root can promote relaxation.
4. **Fruits**: Kiwi and cherries are known for their sleep-enhancing properties.
5. **Leafy Greens**: Spinach and kale are good sources of calcium, which helps with sleep regulation. 
6. **Dark Chocolate**: In moderation, it can boost serotonin and promote better sleep.
