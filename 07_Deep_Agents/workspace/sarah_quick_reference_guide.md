## üßò‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è WEEK 1 YOGA SEQUENCE (15-20 MIN)

1. **Seated Mountain Pose** (1 min) - Sit tall, arms overhead, breathe
2. **Neck Rolls** (2 min) - Gentle circles both directions
3. **Cat-Cow Stretch** (3 min) - On all fours, arch and round back
4. **Seated Forward Bend** (3 min) - Legs extended, reach toward toes
5. **Gentle Twist** (2 min) - Sit tall, twist left and right
6. **Tree Pose** (2 min) - Use chair for balance if needed
7. **Child's Pose** (2 min) - Kneel, sit back, arms forward

### Modifications
- **Low energy**: Do all poses seated in chair
- **Balance issues**: Use wall or chair for support
- **Joint stiffness**: Move gently, don't force
