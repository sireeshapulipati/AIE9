# Week 3 Yoga Sequence (10-20 minutes)

## Daily Schedule:
**Morning Practice (Daily): 10-20 minutes** 

### 1. Seated Cat-Cow Pose  
- **Duration:** 5-7 breaths each pose (1-2 minutes)  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit on the edge of the chair with feet flat on the floor.  
   2. Place your hands on your knees.  
   3. Inhale, arch your back, lifting your head and chest (similar to Cat-Cow).  
   4. Exhale, round your back, tucking your chin (similar to Cat-Cow).  
   5. **Modification:** Move slower or hold a position longer if desired.  

### 2. Chair Forward Bend  
- **Duration:** 1-2 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit tall in your chair and slowly hinge forward, bringing your chest closer to your thighs.  
   2. Let your arms hang down or grasp your ankles.  
   3. Breathe deeply, feeling the release in your lower back.  
   4. **Modification:** Keep your knees bent if necessary.  

### 3. Chair Pigeon Pose  
- **Duration:** 2-3 minutes on each side  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit in your chair, cross your right ankle over your left knee.  
   2. Gently lean forward until you feel a stretch in your right hip.  
   3. Hold and breathe, then switch sides.  
   4. **Modification:** Keep your back straight and lean forward only as far as comfortable.

---

## Meditation Practice (Before or After Yoga):
### Body Scan Meditation 
- **Duration:** 10 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Find a comfortable seated position or lie down.  
   2. Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths.  
   3. Bring your awareness to your feet, noticing any sensations, and gradually move up your body (feet, legs, torso, arms, head).  
   4. Acknowledge any tension and allow it to soften with each exhale.

---

### Tips for Practice:
- Use a cushion or blanket for extra comfort in your chair.  
- Stay mindful and listen to your body, adjusting poses as needed.
