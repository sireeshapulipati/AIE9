# Week 2 Yoga Sequence (10-20 minutes)

## Daily Schedule:
**Morning Practice (Daily): 10-20 minutes** 

### 1. Seated Spinal Twist (Ardha Matsyendrasana)
- **Duration:** 5-7 breaths per side (2-3 minutes)  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit with legs extended, bend your right knee, placing your foot outside your left knee.  
   2. Inhale, lengthen your spine, and exhale as you twist to the right, using your left elbow for support.  
   3. Hold, breathing deeply, before switching sides.  
   4. **Modification:** Instead of twisting, simply stay seated upright if it feels better.  

### 2. Side Stretch (Utwistasan)
- **Duration:** 1 minute per side (2 minutes total)  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Stand or sit, raising one arm overhead and tilting your body to the opposite side.  
   2. Hold for several breaths, then switch sides.  
   3. **Modification:** Perform seated side stretches while sitting in a chair.  

### 3. Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani)
- **Duration:** 2-4 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit next to a wall and carefully swing your legs up, lying back against the floor.  
   2. Relax in this position, arms at your sides.  
   3. **Modification:** If uncomfortable, place a folded blanket under your hips for support.

---

## Meditation Practice (Before or After Yoga):
### Guided Loving-Kindness Meditation 
- **Duration:** 10 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Find a comfortable seated position, close your eyes, and take a few deep breaths.  
   2. Silently repeat phrases of love and kindness towards yourself and others. For example, "May I be happy. May I be safe. May I be healthy. May I live with ease."  
   3. Expand the phrases to loved ones and even to those you find difficult to love.

---

### Tips for Practice:
- Be patient with yourself and make adjustments as needed.  
- Focus on your breath to anchor your practice.  
- Ensure you're comfortable, using props if necessary.
