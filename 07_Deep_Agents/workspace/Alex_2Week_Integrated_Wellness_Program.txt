## Daily Integration Schedule

### Week 1

- Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas and spinach

## Key Workplace Stress Strategies
1. Take regular short breaks
2. Set clear work-hour boundaries
3. Keep workspace organized
4. Delegate when possible
5. Practice mini-meditation when overwhelmed

## Hydration Guidelines
- Start day with water upon waking
- Aim for 8 glasses of water daily
- Herbal teas (chamomile, valerian) in evening
- Limit caffeine after 2 PM

## Success Tips
- **Consistency over perfection**: Missing one day doesn't derail progress
- **Evening routine is crucial**: Start winding down 1 hour before target bedtime
- **Listen to your body**: Modify exercises as needed
- **Meal prep helps**: Prepare ingredients on weekends
- **Track your mood and energy**: Note what works best for you
