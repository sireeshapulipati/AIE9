# Week 4 Yoga Sequence (10-20 minutes)

## Daily Schedule:
**Morning Practice (Daily): 10-20 minutes** 

### 1. Breath of Joy  
- **Duration:** 3-5 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Stand or sit comfortably.  
   2. Inhale deeply through your nose, raising your arms overhead.  
   3. Exhale while bringing your arms down, bending forward slightly.  
   4. Repeat this sequence 5-10 times, varying your speed to find what feels energizing for you.  
   5. **Modification:** If standing is challenging, perform seated by raising arms overhead while being seated.  

### 2. Gentle Flow Sequence  
- **Duration:** 5-10 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Start in Mountain Pose (Tadasana).  
   2. On inhale, extend arms up, then exhale as you fold forward into Forward Bend (Uttanasana).  
   3. Inhale, step back to a gentle Downward Dog, holding for 5 breaths.  
   4. Walk back to Forward Bend, then roll up to standing.  
   5. **Modification:** Use a wall for support if you feel unstable.

- **Instructions:**  
   1. Use a bolster or cushion between your knees and belly.  
   2. Sit back on your heels and rest forward over the bolster.

   4. **Modification:** If needed, open the legs wider to create space in your hips.

## Meditation Practice (Before or After Yoga):
### Visualization for Energy  
- **Duration:** 15 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit in a comfortable position, close your eyes, and take a few deep breaths.  
   2. Visualize a warm light filling your body with energy and positivity.  
   3. Imagine this light dissolving any fatigue and revitalizing your spirit.  
   4. Reflect on your intentions for the day while focusing on this light.

---

### Tips for Practice:
- Ensure your environment is supportive and calming.  
- Feel free to adjust timings based on your energy levels.  
- Consistency is key; practice daily as much as possible.
