# 2-Week Exercise Program for Alex

### Overview
- **Duration**: 2 Weeks
- **Frequency**: 3 Times per Week (Evening)
- **Duration per Session**: 30 Minutes
- **Goals**: Improve Energy Levels and Overall Fitness
- **Level**: Beginner to Intermediate Friendly

### Format
Each session includes:
- Warm-Up: 5 minutes
- Workout: 20 minutes
- Cool Down: 5 minutes

## Weekly Schedule

### Week 1
#### Day 1: Monday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- Arm Circles: 1 min  
- Leg Swings: 1 min (30 seconds each leg)  
- Torso Twists: 1 min  
- March in Place: 2 mins

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps  
2. Push-Ups (knee or standard): 3 sets of 5-10 reps  
3. Dumbbell Rows (use a gallon of water if no weights): 3 sets of 8 reps per arm  
4. Standing Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Standing Forward Bend: 1 min  
- Seated Hamstring Stretch: 1 min  
- Child’s Pose: 1 min  
- Deep Breathing: 2 mins

---  
#### Day 2: Wednesday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- Jumping Jacks: 1 min  
- Side Lunges: 1 min  
- Arm Crosses: 1 min  
- High Knees: 2 mins

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Lunges: 3 sets of 8 reps per leg  
2. Plank: 3 sets of 15-30 seconds  
3. Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 10 reps  
4. Shoulder Press (with light weights): 3 sets of 10 reps

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Figure Four Stretch: 1 min per leg  
- Cat-Cow Stretch: 1 min  
- Side Stretch: 1 min  
- Deep Breathing: 2 mins
  
---  
#### Day 3: Friday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- Butt Kicks: 1 min  
- Walkouts: 1 min  
- Hip Circles: 1 min  
- Arm Circles: 2 mins (1 min forwards, 1 min backwards)

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Step-Ups (use stairs or a sturdy chair): 3 sets of 10 reps per leg  
2. Side Plank (modified if needed): 2 sets of 15-20 seconds per side  
3. Deadlifts (with light weights): 3 sets of 10 reps  
4. Tricep Dips (on a sturdy chair): 3 sets of 8 reps

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Seated Forward Bend: 1 min  
- Lying Spinal Twist: 1 min per side  
- Butterfly Stretch: 1 min  
- Deep Breathing: 2 mins
  
## Week 2
#### Day 4: Monday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- Skaters: 1 min  
- Arm Swings: 1 min  
- Toe Touches: 1 min  
- Marching in Place: 2 mins

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Squat to Press (with light weights): 3 sets of 10 reps  
2. Incline Push-Ups (hands on chair): 3 sets of 5-10 reps  
3. Bent Over Dumbbell Flys: 3 sets of 10 reps  
4. Step-Out Side Lunges: 3 sets of 8 reps per side  

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Piriformis Stretch: 1 min per side  
- Cobra Stretch: 1 min  
- Deep Breathing: 3 mins
 
---  
#### Day 5: Wednesday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- High Knees: 1 min  
- Side Leg Raises: 1 min  
- Circle Hips: 1 min  
- Standing March: 2 mins

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Wall Sit: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds  
2. Reverse Crunches: 3 sets of 10 reps  
3. Side Plank with Leg Raise: 2 sets of 8-10 reps per side  
4. Glute Kickbacks: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Pigeon Stretch: 1 min per leg  
- Child’s Pose: 1 min  
- Neck Stretch: 1 min  
- Deep Breathing: 2 mins

---  
#### Day 6: Friday
**Warm-Up** (5 mins):  
- Jog in Place: 1 min  
- Windmill Arms: 1 min  
- Cat-Cow: 1 min  
- Light Jog to March: 2 mins

**Workout** (20 mins):  
1. Curtsy Lunges: 3 sets of 8 reps per leg  
2. Push-Up with Rotation: 3 sets of 5-8 reps  
3. Dumbbell Curls: 3 sets of 10 reps  
4. Plank to Downward Dog: 3 sets of 5 reps

**Cool Down** (5 mins):  
- Standing Quad Stretch: 1 min per leg  
- Side Bends: 1 min  
- Relaxed Breathing: 3 mins

### Progression Notes:
- Week 2: Gradually increase the repetitions by 1-2 reps or add light weights when comfortable.
- Always focus on maintaining proper form over increasing intensity.
