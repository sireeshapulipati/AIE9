## üîÑ MODIFICATIONS FOR LOW-ENERGY DAYS

### Gentle Alternatives
- **Exercise**: Chair yoga instead of standing poses
- **Walking**: Indoor walking or 10 minutes instead of 20-30
- **Strength**: Skip or do seated exercises only
- **Meals**: Easy options like smoothies, pre-prepped meals

### Listen to Your Body
- Rest is important and part of the plan
- Adjust intensity based on how you feel
- Consistency over perfection
- Some movement is better than none

---

## üéØ SUCCESS STRATEGIES

### Habit Formation
- **Start small**: Begin with 5-10 minutes of each activity
- **Stack habits**: Link new habits to existing routines
- **Track progress**: Visual tracking increases success
- **Celebrate wins**: Acknowledge every small victory

### Energy Management
- **Morning light**: Get sunlight exposure early
- **Consistent sleep**: 7:00 AM wake time supports circadian rhythm
- **Gradual progression**: Increase intensity slowly
- **Nutrition timing**: Eat regularly to maintain steady energy

### Chronic Condition Considerations
- **Flexibility**: Adapt activities to daily energy levels
- **Communication**: Check with healthcare provider about new exercise
- **Self-compassion**: Some days will be harder than others
- **Focus on feelings**: Notice how habits affect energy and mood
