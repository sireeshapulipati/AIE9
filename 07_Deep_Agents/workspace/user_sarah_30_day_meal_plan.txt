30-Day Vegan Meal Planning Strategy for User Sarah (Age 60, Chronic Condition)

### Weekly Meal Themes:
1. **Week 1: Whole Foods & Greens**
   Focus: Leafy greens, whole grains, legumes.
2. **Week 2: Anti-Inflammatory Ingredients**
   Focus: Turmeric, ginger, berries, nuts.
3. **Week 3: Omega-3 Rich Foods**
   Focus: Flaxseeds, walnuts, algae-based supplements.
4. **Week 4: Comforting and Easy-to-Digest Meals**
   Focus: Soups, stews, soft grains.
5. **Week 5: Fermented Foods**
   Focus: Kimchi, sauerkraut, dairy-free yogurt.

### Sample Meal Plans:
**Week 1: Whole Foods & Greens**
- Breakfast: Overnight oats with almond milk, flaxseeds, chia seeds, and fresh berries.
- Lunch: Quinoa salad with spinach, chickpeas, cucumber, avocado, and tahini dressing.
- Dinner: Stir-fried tofu, broccoli, and brown rice with ginger and garlic.
- Snacks: Sliced veggies with hummus, handful of almonds.

**Week 2: Anti-Inflammatory Ingredients**
- Breakfast: Turmeric smoothie with banana, almond milk, and ginger.
- Lunch: Lentil soup with turmeric, carrots, and celery.
- Dinner: Roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, and kale.
- Snacks: Mixed berries, walnuts.

**Week 3: Omega-3 Rich Foods**
- Breakfast: Chia pudding with almond milk, topped with kiwi and nuts.
- Lunch: Spinach salad with avocado, walnuts, and lemon vinaigrette.
- Dinner: Tempeh with Brussels sprouts and quinoa.
- Snacks: Energy balls made with dates and nuts.

**Week 4: Comforting Meals**
- Breakfast: Warm oatmeal with almond butter and banana.
- Lunch: Butternut squash soup with whole grain bread.
- Dinner: Vegetable stew with barley and lentils.
- Snacks: Applesauce, rice cakes with almond butter.

**Week 5: Fermented Foods**
- Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with dairy-free yogurt, topped with granola.
- Lunch: Kimchi fried rice with brown rice.
- Dinner: Taco bowl with black beans, quinoa, and sauerkraut.
- Snacks: Seaweed snacks, fermented pickles.

### Simple Prep Strategies:
- Pre-prepare ingredients and store in airtight containers.
- Utilize one-pot meals for ease.
- Batch cook and freeze meals.
- Use frozen fruits and veggies for convenience.
- Invest in slow cookers for minimal effort.
- Have a list of simple meal options ready.
