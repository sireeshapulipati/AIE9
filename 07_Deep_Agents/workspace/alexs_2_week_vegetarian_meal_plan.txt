# 2-Week Vegetarian Meal Plan for Alex

## Overview
This meal plan is designed for Alex to improve energy levels, support better sleep, and promote mental wellness through a strictly vegetarian diet. Each day includes meals emphasizing nutrient-rich ingredients beneficial for both physical and mental health.

### Nutritional Highlights
- **Foods for Energy:** Oats, quinoa, black beans, lentils, nuts, and seeds.
- **Foods for Better Sleep:** Cherries, bananas, leafy greens, and whole grains.
- **Foods for Mental Wellness:** Avocados, blueberries, spinach, walnuts, and dark chocolate.

## Meal Plan
### Week 1
**Day 1**
- **Breakfast:** Overnight oats with chia seeds, almond milk, berries, and walnuts.
  - **Prep Tip:** Prepare overnight oats the night before.
- **Lunch:** Quinoa salad with black beans, corn, bell peppers, and lime dressing.
- **Dinner:** Lentil stew with carrots, celery, and spices served with whole grain bread.
- **Snacks:** Greek yogurt with honey and almonds.

**Day 2**
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond butter, and flaxseeds.
- **Lunch:** Hummus and veggie wrap with whole wheat tortilla.
- **Dinner:** Stir-fried tofu with broccoli and brown rice.
- **Snacks:** Celery sticks with peanut butter.

**Day 3**
- **Breakfast:** Chia pudding with almond milk and topped with sliced bananas.
- **Lunch:** Mixed greens with chickpeas, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette.
- **Dinner:** Stuffed bell peppers with quinoa and black beans.
- **Snacks:** Apple slices with almond butter.

**Day 4**
- **Breakfast:** Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs.
- **Lunch:** Tomato and basil soup with a side salad.
- **Dinner:** Vegetable curry with lentils and brown rice.
- **Snacks:** Dark chocolate and mixed nuts.

**Day 5**
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie bowl with spinach, banana, and granola.
- **Lunch:** Quinoa and roasted vegetable bowl.
- **Dinner:** Zucchini noodles with marinara sauce and lentil meatballs.
- **Snacks:** Cottage cheese with pineapple.

**Day 6**
- **Breakfast:** Oatmeal topped with almond slices and berries.
- **Lunch:** Buddha bowl with brown rice, kale, chickpeas, and tahini dressing.
- **Dinner:** Cauliflower tacos with avocado sauce.
- **Snacks:** Trail mix.

**Day 7**
- **Breakfast:** Pancakes made with oat flour, served with peanut butter and bananas.
- **Lunch:** Greek salad with feta cheese and olives.
- **Dinner:** Spinach and cheese stuffed shells with marinara sauce.
- **Snacks:** Bell pepper strips with hummus.

## Meal Prep Tips
1. **Batch Cooking:** Cook grains like quinoa and rice in bulk to use throughout the week.
2. **Prepping Snacks:** Portion out nuts, yogurt, and cut vegetables for easy grab-and-go snacks.
3. **Meal Storage:** Use airtight containers to store prepped meals in the fridge for freshness.
4. **Sunday Prep:** Dedicate a few hours on Sunday to prepare meals and snacks for the week ahead.

## Conclusion
This meal plan provides a balance of nutrients and flavors to enhance Alex's energy levels, support restful sleep, and promote mental well-being. By sticking to this plan and shopping list, Alex can maintain a healthy vegetarian lifestyle.
