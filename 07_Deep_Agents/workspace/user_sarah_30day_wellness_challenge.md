# Sarah's 30-Day Wellness Challenge
*Designed for Increasing Energy & Improving Mood*

## Challenge Overview
This gentle, comprehensive wellness challenge is specifically designed for you, Sarah, taking into account your chronic condition, age (60), and preferences for vegan diet and morning routines. The focus is on sustainable habits that gradually build energy and improve mood.

## Daily Structure Template
**Morning Routine (20-30 minutes)**
- Hydration + Gratitude (5 minutes)
- Gentle Yoga/Movement (10-20 minutes) 
- Breathing/Meditation (5-15 minutes)

**Throughout the Day**
- Energy-supportive meals and snacks
- Mood-boosting micro-habits
- Social connection opportunities

**Evening**
- Reflection and planning (5 minutes)

---

## Week 1: Gentle Foundations
**Theme**: Establishing consistent morning routine and energy-supporting nutrition

### Daily Habits:
1. **Morning Hydration**: Drink a glass of room temperature water upon waking
2. **Gentle Stretching**: 5-10 minutes of neck, shoulder, and back stretches
3. **Breathing Exercise**: 3 minutes of deep breathing for mood and energy
4. **Daily Gratitude**: Write one thing you're grateful for

### Yoga & Movement (10 minutes daily):
- Cat-Cow Pose: 5-7 breaths each position
- Child's Pose: 1-2 minutes with cushion support
- Seated Forward Bend: 2-3 minutes (modify as needed)
- **Meditation**: Simple Breath Awareness (5 minutes)

### Nutrition Focus: Whole Foods & Greens
- **Breakfast**: Overnight oats with berries and nuts
- **Lunch**: Quinoa salad with mixed greens, chickpeas, and tahini dressing
- **Dinner**: Stir-fried tofu with seasonal vegetables and brown rice
- **Snacks**: Apple slices with almond butter, handful of mixed nuts

### Energy Tracking: Rate your energy and mood daily (1-5 scale)

---

## Week 2: Building Energy and Mood
**Theme**: Expanding routines and anti-inflammatory nutrition

### Additional Habits:
1. **Increased Stretching**: Extend to 10-15 minutes
2. **Short Walk**: 5-10 minutes outdoors (or chair exercises indoors)
3. **Energy Snack**: Healthy snack post-walk
4. **Mindfulness Break**: 5 minutes focusing on your senses

### Yoga & Movement (15 minutes daily):
- Seated Spinal Twist: 5-7 breaths per side
- Side Stretch: 1 minute per side
- Legs Up the Wall: 2-4 minutes
- **Meditation**: Guided Loving-Kindness (10 minutes)

### Nutrition Focus: Anti-Inflammatory Ingredients
- **Key additions**: Turmeric, ginger, leafy greens, berries
- **Sample meals**: Turmeric smoothie, lentil soup with anti-inflammatory spices
- **Prep strategy**: Batch cook soups and stews for easy reheating

---

## Week 3: Diversification and Chair Yoga Options
**Theme**: New habits and accessible movement for low-energy days

### Additional Habits:
1. **Guided Exercise**: 10-15 minute gentle workout video
2. **Mood-Boosting Playlist**: Uplifting music during stretches/walks
3. **Social Connection**: 5-minute chat with friend/family member
4. **Evening Reflection**: 5 minutes reflecting on day's positives

### Yoga & Movement (15-20 minutes daily):
- **Chair Yoga Options** (for low-energy days):
  - Seated Cat-Cow: 5-7 breaths each
  - Chair Forward Bend: 1-2 minutes
  - Chair Pigeon Pose: 2-3 minutes each side
- **Meditation**: Body Scan (10 minutes)

### Nutrition Focus: Omega-3 Rich Foods
- **Key foods**: Chia seeds, flaxseeds, walnuts, hemp hearts
- **Sample addition**: Chia pudding breakfast, walnut-sprinkled salads
- **Timing**: Include omega-3s in every meal for sustained energy

**Theme**: Solidifying habits and advanced energy techniques

### Advanced Habits:
1. **Extended Workout**: Gradually increase to 20 minutes (if energized)
2. **Snack Prep**: Prepare energy-boosting snacks for the week
3. **Daily Mini Challenges**: Try something new each day
4. **Weekly Review**: 10 minutes reviewing energy and mood patterns

### Yoga & Movement (20 minutes daily):
- Breath of Joy: 3-5 minutes
- Gentle Flow Sequence: 5-10 minutes
- Supported Child's Pose: 3-5 minutes
- **Meditation**: Visualization for Energy (15 minutes)

### Nutrition Focus: Easy-to-Digest Comfort Foods
- **Theme**: Comforting, nourishing meals that support digestion
- **Sample meals**: Warm oatmeal with stewed fruits, butternut squash soup
- **Strategy**: Focus on cooked vs. raw foods for easier digestion

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Low Energy Days:
- Switch to chair yoga sequences
- Reduce workout time to 5-10 minutes
- Choose pre-made snacks and simple meals
- Focus on breathing exercises over movement

### Missed Days:
- Don't aim for perfection - consistency over intensity
- Restart with previous week's routine if needed
- Use "minimum viable" versions (2-minute stretch, 1 gratitude note)

### Energy Fluctuations:
- Track patterns in your journal
- Adjust meal timing if needed
- Consider rest days between more active days
- Stay hydrated throughout the day
