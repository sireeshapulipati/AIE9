# Week 1 Yoga Sequence (10-20 minutes)

## Daily Schedule:
**Morning Practice (Daily): 10-20 minutes** 

### 1. Cat-Cow Pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)
- **Duration:** 5-7 breaths each pose (1-2 minutes)  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Start on all fours, knees under hips, hands under shoulders.  
   2. Inhale, arch your back, lifting your head and tailbone (Cow Pose).  
   3. Exhale, round your back, tucking your chin and tailbone (Cat Pose).  
   4. Flow gently between these two positions, synchronizing breath with movement.  
   5. **Modification:** If on knees is uncomfortable, perform seated cat-cow while sitting on a chair.  

### 2. Childâ€™s Pose (Balasana)
- **Duration:** 1-2 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. From all fours, sit back on your heels, stretching your arms forward or resting them by your sides.  
   2. Rest your forehead on the mat, closing your eyes and taking deep breaths.  
   3. **Modification:** Use a bolster or cushion under your torso for support.  

### 3. Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)
- **Duration:** 2-3 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Sit with legs extended in front of you.
   2. Inhale, lengthen your spine, and exhale as you hinge forward from the hips, reaching toward your feet.  
   3. Hold the pose where you feel comfortable, breathing deeply.  
   4. **Modification:** Bend your knees slightly or use a strap around your feet.

## Meditation Practice (Before or After Yoga):
### Breath Awareness 
- **Duration:** 5 minutes  
- **Instructions:**  
   1. Find a comfortable seated position, either on a chair or the floor.  
   2. Close your eyes, and bring your attention to your breath.  
   3. Notice the sensation of breathing in and out, focusing on each inhale and exhale.  
   4. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to your breath.

---

### Tips for Practice:
- Always listen to your body, and take breaks if needed.  
- Practice in a calm environment to enhance relaxation.  
- You can choose to hold poses for longer if comfortable.
